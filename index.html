About us

Free Tech Institute (FREETI) is a non-profit think tank. We advocate for a libertarian philosophy focused on technology and entrepreneurship, particularly in the field of ICT (Fintech). Through our programs, we discuss topics such as privacy protection, prosperity through a free market on the web, intellectual property rights, cryptocurrency, anti-surveillance, responsible internet use, and individual freedom. Our aim is to foster a more liberated Burundian web environment through education and networking.



Recent achievements

Crypto awareness drive in Burundi

Trezor Academy Burundi, a collaboration between Free Tech Institute and Trezor, aimed to expedite Bitcoin adoption in Burundi. It consisted of three consecutive Saturday meetups, educating youth about cryptocurrencies, their advantages, and practical applications. The series of meetups covered "Why Bitcoin?", "How to Use Bitcoin?", and "What Bitcoiners Should Do?" with insightful presentations, practical exercises,and certificate awards.

LeaderNet Program

The LeaderNet Program, initiated by Free Tech Institute (FREETI) in January 2023, aims to empower and educate high school students in Burundi on liberty, the responsible and effective use of digital technologies. FREETI focuses on fostering essential skills for socioprofessional development while promoting the responsible use of information and communication technologies (ICT).

Talk Tech Liberty Program

The year 2023 was special for our organization as we reached more than 60,000 people through the Talk Tech Liberty program. This year, we produced 4 educational videos, 34 articles, and 546 informative Card news that helped people understand international and local issues such as the adoption of cryptocurrencies, protecting privacy online, online security and other current topics within our area of expertise.
