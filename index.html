About Me

Advaxe Ndayisenga is a Burundian Web and Software Developer, specializing in open source technologies and Bitcoin development. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the East African Leadership Institute, he brings over five years of experience in building digital solutions for businesses, NGOs, and government clients. As the Director and Business Development Manager at LA TECH BURUNDI COMPANY, Advaxe also serves as a Fullstack Developer. He previously held the same role at Media Box Burundi S.A, where he led projects focused on digital transformation for institutional partners. His technical expertise spans WordPress, Prestashop, SEO auditing, and social media marketing. A committed community leader, he plays an active role as a trainer, speaker, and event organizer with Students For Liberty, and serves as a representative of Great Lakes Marketing and Communication (MARCOM). Passionate about innovation and empowerment, he is a recognized UPG Sustainability Ambassador, having graduated as both a UPG Leader (2022) and UPG Biashara Entrepreneur (2023). Advaxe continually sharpens his skills through global learning platforms including Atlas Network Academy, FutureLearn, Lurn, OpenClassrooms, Microverse, and the British Council. His focus includes emotional intelligence, time management, and project leadership. Fluent in Kirundi, English, French, and Kiswahili, Advaxe brings a multilingual and multicultural perspective to every project. Through his work, he is committed to fostering open technology, sustainable development, and digital liberty in African communities and beyond.
